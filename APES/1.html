Making Smaller Margins

With smaller margins, you are able to fit more content on each page.

This extended content allows one to reduce paper printed, however while continuing to keep their content.

Changing the default margins from 1.25 inches to 1 inch can reduce the amount of paper you use by up to eight percent.
